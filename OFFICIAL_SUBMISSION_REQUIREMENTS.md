## Eligibility blockers

- Residence/domicile must be in an OpenAI API-supported country or territory and not legally excluded.
- Rules explicitly exclude Brazil, Quebec, Russia, Crimea, Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, other legally prohibited/OFAC-designated locations, promotion entities and their employees/agents/immediate family or household, judges and their employers, affiliates, involved organizers, and real/apparent conflicts of interest.
- Project must be new during submission period, or meaningfully extended with Codex and/or GPT-5.6 after July 13, 2026, 9:00 AM PT. Pre-existing work needs clear dated evidence distinguishing new work.
- Third-party SDK/API/data use must be authorized and license-compliant.
